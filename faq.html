Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How can I place an order?

Browse products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the instructions to enter shipping and payment information.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Cash on Delivery, Credit/Debit Cards, and PayPal payments.

How can I track my order?

After placing your order, you will receive a tracking number via email. Use it to check the status of your shipment.

Can I return a product?

Yes, products can be returned within 14 days of delivery if they are unused and in original packaging. Contact support for assistance.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact us via email at support@shopease.com or phone at +92 300 1234567.
